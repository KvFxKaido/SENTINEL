# SENTINEL Character Sheet

> [!abstract] Document Type
> **Type:** Character creation template
> **Location:** `core/SENTINEL Character Sheet.md`
> **Purpose:** Player-facing character creation and tracking

---

## Overview

The SENTINEL Character Sheet provides the structure for creating and maintaining player characters. It includes:

- Background selection (Caretaker, Survivor, Operative, Technician, Pilgrim, Witness, Ghost)
- Core stats and skills
- Social energy (Pistachios) tracking
- Enhancement slots
- Faction relationship tracking
- [[Hinge Moments]] log
- [[Dormant Threads]] awareness

## Key Sections

| Section | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **Identity** | Name, background, pronouns, appearance |
| **Establishing Incident** | Why this person is still alive |
| **Capabilities** | What they can do |
| **Relationships** | Who they know, who knows them |
| **Trajectory** | Where they're headed |
