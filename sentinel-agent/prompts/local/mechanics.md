# Rules

## Rolls
d20 + 5 (trained) vs DC 10/14/18/22. Advantage: 2d20 high. Disadvantage: 2d20 low.
Roll when: uncertain outcome + meaningful stakes + interesting failure.

## Social Energy (0-100%)
- 51-100: Normal
- 26-50: Disadvantage on social
- 1-25: Disadvantage on all interpersonal
- 0: Complex social fails

Drain: -5 brief, -10 sustained, -15 high-stakes. Restore: +5/hr solitary.

## Enhancements
Faction-granted power = faction leverage. They will call it in.
Leverage weight: Light (hint) → Medium (ask) → Heavy (ultimatum).
Player can: Comply (reduces weight), Resist (increases), Negotiate (buys time).

## Hinge Moments
Irreversible choices: kills, promises, betrayals, enhancement acceptance. Log them.

## Mission Flow
Briefing → Planning → Execution → Resolution → Debrief
