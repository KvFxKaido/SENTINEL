## Overview

The SENTINEL Playbook contains the complete rules for running the tabletop roleplaying game. It covers:

## Core Systems

- **Social Energy (Pistachios)** — Emotional bandwidth tracking
- **[[Hinge Moments]]** — Irreversible identity-defining choices
- **[[Dormant Threads]]** — Delayed consequences
- **Enhancements** — Faction-granted power with strings attached
- **NPC Disposition** — How NPCs react based on history

## Session Structure

1. **Briefing** — Situation and stakes
2. **Investigation** — Gather information
3. **Interpretation** — Understand the situation
4. **Choice** — Make decisions
5. **Consequence** — Live with the results
6. **Debrief** — Process and record

## Philosophy

> [!quote] Design Principle
> "This game is not about min-max optimization, combat dominance, or binary morality. It's about navigating competing truths, sustaining integrity under pressure, and choosing consequences you can live with."
